ArmatureComponent

An ArmatureComponent plays animations and generates pose (deformation) matrices for 3D models.

Usage

Use PoolProxy::New() to allocate a new ArmatureComponent.

Then use the SetModel(UID("_model_name_here")) to set the model that the ArmatureComponent will play animations for. Then call the Init() method to start the component. It will load the model in the background.

Finally call the GetPosePtr() method to get a pointer that you have to put into you RenderComponent's SetPose() method. This will link the components together.

You can use PlayAnimation() method to play animations or SetBoneKeyframe() method to generate animations procedurally.

When the ArmatureComponent is not needed anymore, call the Uninit() method to remove it from the pose list.

Use PoolProxy::Delete() to deallocate the unneeded ArmatureComponent.

Note

Sometimes not good:

  • If you call the StartAnimation() before the model is loaded, the animation will not play.
  • If you Uninit() your ArmatureComponent before your RenderComponent, call the SetPose() on your RenderComponent with nullptr argument.
